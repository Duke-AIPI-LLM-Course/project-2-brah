Industry Affiliates
University-Industry Relationships, Simplified
Let’s face it—research universities are complex. They can be tough to engage and even more difficult to navigate.
At Duke, our fresh approach accelerates mutually beneficial relationships with industry. Each structured industry affiliate program is a gateway with a clear line of sight to the thought leaders and research talent working in the topic areas most relevant to you.
