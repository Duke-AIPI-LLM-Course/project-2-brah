Annual Security and Fire Safety Reports:
The Annual Security Report and Annual Fire Safety Report include institutional policies concerning campus safety and security, as well as statistics for the previous three years concerning reported crimes that occurred on Duke University property and on public property adjacent to campus as well as fires that have occurred in residence halls. A copy of these reports can be obtained by contacting the Duke Police Department at +1 919-684-4602, or writing 502 Oregon Street, Durham, NC 27708 USA, or by accessing:
