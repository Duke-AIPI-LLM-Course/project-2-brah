Industry Engagement
Go Farther, Faster
Innovation is no accident. It’s a habit—a cycle of intentional behavior that actively identifies challenges and designs implementable solutions.
Partner With Duke
Duke Engineering develops lasting partnerships with organizations that, like Duke, want to push the innovation cycle faster and go farther.
Our partners find ways to improve their organizations—and make a positive impact on the next generation of engineering leaders—through unique programs engaging our high-impact research faculty, skilled staff and talented students.
Become a partner. Let’s work together toward a better future.

Duke Computer Science, Pratt School of Engineering, and Career Center held the Fall TechConnect 2018 on Tuesday, September 25, 2018 at the Fitzpatrick Center. TechConnect is an opportunity for students to do real micro interviews with recruiters in the tech sector and companies that hire technology professionals. Companies sending recruiters included Microsoft, Google, Kohler, Next, Facebook, HondaJet, InfiniaML, and Garmin.
