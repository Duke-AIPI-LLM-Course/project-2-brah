Graduate Student Resources
Grad School Isn’t Easy. We Get That.
Duke isn’t a place for sink-or-swim attitudes.
When you join our Pratt School of Engineering community, you create bonds that go deeper than projects, papers and performance. We’re invested in your success, not only professionally but personally.
We’re about building a support network that works to keep everyone afloat and striving for their goals. We’re about making connections, lasting and beneficial.
Support for the Whole Person
Communicating Effectively
From mastering small talk to preparing effective presentations, our communications consultants coach graduate students to write and speak with clarity. Click to browse a menu of services.
Engaging Socially
Our warm and welcoming graduate student community is an active one. Click to get a glimpse of our curated events that promote community, strengthen networks and open career possibilities.
Supporting Authentically
Members of our grad student support team have earned certifications from the National Board for
Health and Wellness Coaching. Click to book 1:1 time with them.
Building Community, at Every Level
Engineering Graduate Student Council
Represents and convenes Duke Engineering master’s and PhD students. Beyond advising the school and organizing outreach service, EGSC is the keeper of an important tradition: Friday afternoon “Pratt Chat” socials on Harrington Engineering Quad.
Engineering Master’s Programs Student Advisory Board
A body to represent the voice of our master’s students. Board members provide valuable insights and ideas and plan events and activities that build and strengthen the community.
Departmental Grad Student Associations
Each of Duke Engineering’s academic departments host permanent committees that allow graduate students to advise faculty and staff on their needs and to work collaboratively to build a deep sense of community.
Duke’s Resources
The Graduate School at Duke maintains a comprehensive list of campus-wide resources for physical and mental well being.
